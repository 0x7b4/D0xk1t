About

When conducting a penetration test, mass mailer attacks are great for sending large amounts of bulk emails. These enable security analysts to collect a multitude of data, whether it is through passive attacks, where attackers aim to gain simple information like phone numbers and addresses, or much more aggressive ones, such as through spear phishing to gain much more sensitive and valuable information such as passwords.
